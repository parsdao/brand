Usage Guidelines

  • Minimum Size: 32px for icon, 120px for logo with detail
  • Clear Space: Maintain 1/4 logo width margin around logo
  • Dark Backgrounds: Use full color or white variant
  • Light Backgrounds: Use monochrome dark variant
  • Colored Backgrounds: Use white variant on blues, mono on yellows
  • Never: Rotate, distort, add effects, or change colors
